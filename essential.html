What are essential oils?
They are nature’s defence mechanism for plants and cells. Essential oils in plants protecting them from mould, pests, fungus, heat etc as well as aiding in pollination. They are highly concentrated, 50-70 times for effective than herbs. One drop of Peppermint oil is equal to 28 cups of Peppermint tea. Essential oils have been used throughout ancient times, prior to modern medicine these were the used for many physical and spiritual purposes. Using essential oils today, for therapeutic purposes, as a natural solution to health, its paramount that you choose a trusted brand. A company who source their oils through a global botanical network of growers and distillers; a company who use third party testing, so that the purity and potency of the oils can be stamped as certified pure therapeutic grade.

What does that mean, certified pure therapeutic grade? This means that the oils you are using, are noted for their specific chemical composition and have been tested to verify their purity.

Why are Essential Oils good for you?
Essential oils are natural,volatile aromatic compounds extracted from plants. These natural chemical compounds are not synthetic and contain no additives, which could have side effects to our physical and/or mental wellbeing. They are literally “gifts of the Earth”….
